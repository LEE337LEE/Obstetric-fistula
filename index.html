Services delivered

Counseling the Obstetric Fistula Client: Counseling is an integral part of comprehensive fistula care services.The curriculum guides nurses, midwives, and physicians to help their clients to make informed and voluntary decisions about their care.
The curriculum addresses counseling needs related to fistula prevention, treatment, and social reintegration. Health care providers can also find information about how to engage with the clientâ€™s family and how to link the client to supportive organizations in the community.

Counseling the Traumatic Fistula Client: This complements the counseling curriculum to address the physical, social, and psychological impacts specific to traumatic fistula.
The supplement also provides information on how health care providers working with traumatic fistula clients can seek support for themselves.
